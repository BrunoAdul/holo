Innovative Public Health Research
The group excels in cutting-edge research
focused on non-communicable and infectious
diseases. By employing an interdisciplinary
approach, it advances public health knowledge,
particularly in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Holo Global Health Research Institute is a beacon of
innovation in public health. Our experts delve into
the complexities of non-communicable and
infectious diseases, particularly within Sub-Saharan
Africa, blending field research with cutting-edge
technology.
